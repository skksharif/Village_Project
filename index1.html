Pedakallepalli

Mopidevi

Pingali Venkayya was an Indian freedom fighter. He was the
designer of the Indian National Flag. He spent most of his
childhood in Pedakallepalli

Sri Durga Nageswara Swamy Pedakallepalli Temple has unique significance
in many respects. It is located in Mopidevi mandal of Machilipatnam
revenue division.Pilgrims and devotees visit the shrine in large
numbers on the eve of Maha Shivaratri festival every year.
